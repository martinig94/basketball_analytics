# <span class="section-badge">H</span> Final Remarks

## Overview

Beyond standard team and player profiling, additional quantitative analysis can be conducted to support scouting, game preparation, and in-game adjustments. These metrics focus on **lineups interaction**, **player synergy**, and **high-leverage possessions**, providing deeper insight into performance beyond traditional box score indicators.

---

## Lineups Analysis

Evaluation of five-man units to identify the most effective and most vulnerable combinations.

Key outputs:  
- Offensive Rating / Defensive Rating per lineup  
- Net Rating (on-court impact)  
- Usage of starting vs bench units  
- Lineup stability under different game contexts  
- Spacing efficiency and shot quality by unit  

---

## Duo Analysis

Assessment of two-player combinations to evaluate on-court synergy.

Key outputs:  
- Net Rating for player pairs  
- Assist connections between primary creators and finishers  
- Pick-and-roll efficiency by specific duo  
- On/off shooting splits when paired  
- Defensive compatibility in switch and hedge schemes  

---

## Clutch Time Analysis

Performance in end-game situations (last 4 minutes, score < 10 points).

Key outputs:  
- Clutch Offensive and Defensive Rating  
- Primary shot creators in late possessions  
- Shot distribution changes under pressure  
- Turnover rate in high-pressure possessions  
- Free throw generation and efficiency in close games  

---

## Summary

These advanced metrics complement standard scouting by focusing on **interaction effects and situational performance**, supporting lineup optimization, rotation decisions, and end-game strategy.
