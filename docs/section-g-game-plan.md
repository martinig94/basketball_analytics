## Top 5 Emphasis Points

**1 — Contain Wade Baldwin IV in Pick & Roll**

Baldwin is the offensive engine (14.4 PPG, 5.5 APG). He prefers right-side PnR with Birch rolling to the rim. Use Hedge/Show coverages to disrupt his rhythm. Force him left, shade his driving lane, and mix coverages to keep him off balance. He is a streaky shooter (27.7% 3P%) — do not go fully under screens but be disciplined, not reckless.

**2 — Close Out Hard on Biberovic, Never Relax**

Biberovic is their most efficient 3-point shooter (39.2% on 5 attempts per game; 60% 3P% in the last 5 games). He relocates well off screens and has a quick trigger. Top-lock him on the weak side, be physical on any screen action, and force him to dribble rather than shoot.

**3 — Exploit De Colo and Horton Tucker Defensively**

De Colo has limited lateral quickness and is often protected within the scheme. Force him into on-ball situations via PnR or ISO. Horton Tucker can be exploited with sequential off-ball actions that test his reads. These are the two most attackable defenders in their rotation.

**4 — Attack Khem Birch Away from the Rim**

Birch is their primary roll man and rim protector, but he struggles when pulled out of the paint. Use pick-and-pop actions and short roll reads to draw him away from his comfort zone. With Birch out of position, the paint becomes accessible.

**5 — Push Pace and Attack Transition**

Fenerbahce allows 9.3 fast-break points per game and has gone 0-5 in their last 5 games. They prefer to control tempo. Generate pace off defensive rebounds and made baskets — run secondary break before their defense sets up, particularly targeting De Colo who is slow recovering. They concede 2.6 transition fouls per game leading to free throws.
